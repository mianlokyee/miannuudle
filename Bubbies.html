Bubbies

"Bubbies" is a brand of hair treatment. The name combines the words “bubbles” and “buddies”. Everyone becomes bubbles and be friends with the bubbles when bathing. We can enter a new place through imagination and forget our daily stress.

The Packaging

The packaging uses circular form which likes bubble. The bottle can directly eject bubble form of shampoo. The users can easily use the bubbles to make their own creations. The label is shape of cloud and the flavors are named with weather.

Think and Relax.

Everyone in the city bears lots of duties and stress. Bubbies promotes imagination and relaxation. We can do what we want when we are using Bubbies for washing their hair, just freely and relaxingly. Advertisements are placed in the different transportation, where people is rushing and feeling stressful for work.

We creates patterns which can be regrouped for different meaning. This is a way for imagination, which is the purpose of this brand. There is font made for Bubbies with circular shape like bubbles. The colour scheme is also pale and mild which enhances the relaxing feeling of the brand.
