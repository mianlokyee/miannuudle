Movie
Logotype

This is a project of redesigning the movie logotype. I choose the film 'Weathering With You' to be the movie analyzed.

Study of Weather.

Studied to the shape of rain and sunshine, the typography transforms weather to lines. It is in coherence with the feeling and graphic of the poster. The '天' and '子‘ represent rainy weather and '氣‘ and ‘之' represent sunny. It is related to the flow of the plot in the movie.  Also, the halo effect in the outer space of the typography in related to the halo of the rain in the movie.
