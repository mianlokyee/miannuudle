Warehouse
Way-finding

This Project collaborates with The Warehouse Teenage Club Ltd. . We helped them to create a way-finding system in Warehouse. Warehouse is a youth organization in Hong Kong. It is a monument, the former Police station in Aberdeen.

My concept is based on the feeling of Warehouse. Warehouse is a mixture of old and new, which is the old brick building and a new area of teenagers. Therefore, I used 80s Retro colour with earth colour for my whole way-finding systems. The concept of the symbol is based on the change from old to new, which is mixing the brick-like pattern with graffiti style of words. The map is using zoning systems to relocate the rooms with different colours and numbers, in order to make user easier to find the location.

The materials used for the symbol are plastic wood and hard plastic. The plastic wood is water-resistant, which could be placed in some outdoor area.

The colours of plastic, purple, light blue, and pink are the same as the zoning colours of the block. This can make the users easier to spot the location. They are made by hard plastic and convey energetic feelings.
