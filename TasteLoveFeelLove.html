Taste Love,
Feel Love

This film is about the meal and the family love. Its name is "Taste Love, Feel Love". In Chinese, it is called " 嚐愛 " which means "Taste the love" and " 嚐 "(eat), have the same pronunciation with ” 常 "(always). I want to grab this meaning for taste the love and your family always love you.

Give thanks to family.
Your family make meals for you everyday. This is the expressions of love from the family.
We should feel this love always and give thanks to them.

I use the rice with Qizi to link the whole story between mum and the son.
The son doesn't like the 'weird' action done by his mum. However, he do not know the real meaning of a mum putting Qizi into the rice. The Qizi is representing the hope and blessing og health by mum to her son.
