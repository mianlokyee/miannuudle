Ariana

Ariana is a monthly scientific magazine series. It is an magazine that is shading light on social issues in Asia. This project is to analysis the original magazine spreads and find out the problem in the layout. Then, a redesign of inner pages with a better solution should be made to replace the original design.

The Original Design

I found some problems in the original design.The visual style of hand-drawing pictures are not in the same style with the computer graphics. The pictures are also too simple with limited colours. The cover story is dull. The word spacing should be adjusted also, as the body text is using justify align. The word spacing varies. Readability is affected. 

The Redesign

As it is about love and sex, the whole colour scheme used is in warm colours, such as pink, yellow and orange-red, to show the passion and romantic mood. The post-modern design are adopted to show balance of futuristic (scientific magazine) and passionate style (sex topic). I also separate the Chinese and English version to improve the readability, in which the problem I have defined in the previous stage.
