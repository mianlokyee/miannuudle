Type Specimen Book

Centaur is a humanistic typeface which is elegant and old-styled. I am interested to its story and the name of it is beautiful. Also,  I like the stroke of the 'Q' as its tail is long and the whole typeface is elegant. This project is to demonstrate the background of Centaur and its autonomy.

The Old Typeface

As I want to achieve the elegant feeling, I used symmetrical balance on my cover, with a medieval style pattern. The Renaissance's colour scheme is used. This is a journey to explore Centaur. Therefore, I designed a door with a hole to show the suitcase inside. I made the endpaper using image of centaur and some medieval patterns. The sectional pages are used medieval patterns as well. The book is in coptic binding to enhance its old-styled feeling.
