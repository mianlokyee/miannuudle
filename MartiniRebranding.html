Martini
Rebranding

“Let’s Make Time” is a new brand experience of Martini beyond traditional advertising. The brand targeted the middle-aged group but now it wants to make it more vitalized. We encourage people to spend quality time for friends during Martini Time and make the brand close to teenagers more.

New Promotion Strategy

We use three colours to symbolise three timing for drinking Martini, which means Martini can be enjoy at anytime. Social media are also the platform that can keep in touch with teenagers. People can upload their photos with Martini and tag us. A 20 seconds Instagram Ad is created also.
